Technical Specification

Project 001 – Core Intents

Foundation (1–3) for Automation Flow

Intent 1

Buy Fish (طلب أسماك)

Purpose: Enable direct ordering of fish products through a structured WhatsApp flow.
  • Greeting & Entry Detection
  • Product Categories
  • Item Selection
  • Weight Selection
  • Preparation Notes
  • Order Summary
Intent 2

Ask Prices (الاستفسار عن الأسعار)

Purpose: Price transparency integrated within the ordering experience.
  • Price shown during item selection
  • Weight-based pricing logic
  • No standalone price list
Intent 3

Ask About Delivery (الاستفسار عن التوصيل)

Purpose: Clarify delivery availability, cost, and timing before payment.
  • Location or address capture
  • Delivery availability validation
  • Delivery fee & ETA confirmation
  • Order summary update
