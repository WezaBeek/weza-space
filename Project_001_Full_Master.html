Project 001 – Full Master Reference

Complete WHAT Foundation + Intents 1–4 (Draft)

Phase WHAT – Master Definition

Rule: Any and every message from a customer MUST map to a specific intent. No dead ends.

Customer Journey (External Path):

Entry → Greeting → Menu → Selection → Quantity → Summary → Payment → Confirmation → Preparing → Out for Delivery → Delivered → Mandatory Feedback

* Feedback is mandatory: essential for trust, reviews, and social proof.

Intent 1

Buy Fish (طلب أسماك)

  • Greeting & Intent Detection
  • Category selection
  • Item selection
  • Weight options (1kg, 1.5kg, 2kg, 2.5kg, 3–5kg)
  • Preparation notes
  • Order summary & confirmation
Intent 2

Ask Prices (الأسعار)

  • Prices shown during selection only
  • Weight-based pricing logic
  • No standalone price list (integrated flow)
Intent 3

Ask About Delivery (التوصيل)

  • Capture location/address
  • Validate delivery availability
  • Confirm delivery fee & ETA
  • Update order summary
Intent 4 DRAFT

Payment Questions (الدفع)

  • Explain payment methods clearly
  • Available: Digital wallets, cards
  • COD Policy: Not available for new customers
  • Future COD only for trusted returning customers
  • Always reassure before payment trigger
  • Link payment explanation back to active order
